# Counter-Offer Script

**Company:** {COMPANY}
**Current Offer:** {OFFER_VND} / ${OFFER_USD}
**Target:** {TARGET_VND} / ${TARGET_USD}
**Confidence:** {CONFIDENCE}

---

## Standard Script (~70 seconds)

### Opening (10 seconds)

"Thank you so much for the offer. I'm excited about the opportunity to join {COMPANY} and work with the team."

### Transition (5 seconds)

"Before I accept, I wanted to discuss the compensation package."

### Data Anchor (30 seconds)

"I've researched the market rate for this role in {LOCATION} with my {EXPERIENCE} years of experience. Based on ITViec and industry data, comparable positions typically offer {MARKET_RANGE}."

### Counter (10 seconds)

"I'd be comfortable accepting {TARGET_VND} (approximately ${TARGET_USD}), which I believe reflects both the market rate and my qualifications."

### Close (15 seconds)

"I'm very excited about this opportunity and confident this is fair market value. Would you be able to take this to your compensation team?"

---

**Total speaking time:** ~70 seconds
**Tone:** Enthusiastic, professional, data-driven

## Tips

- Practice out loud 3-5 times before the call
- Have your research data ready to cite specific sources
- Speak slowly and confidently
- Pause after stating your counter to let them respond
